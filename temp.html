School of Social Sciences

School of Social Sciences comments:

DROP: The deadline to drop courses in the School of Social Sciences is the end of Week 2 by 5:00PM. Drops can be made in WebReg.

ADD: The deadline to add courses in the School of Social Sciences is the end of week 2 by 5:00PM. Adds can be made in WebReg.

CHANGE: The deadline to change grade option or variable units via WebReg for majors in the School of Social Sciences is the end of Week 2 by 5:00PM. Change of grade options or variable units beginning weeks 3-6 must be made through Student Access. Courses for the School's math requirement and major requirements must be completed with letter grade only.

Due to the volume of majors and incoming new students, enrollment in some School of Social Sciences courses will be restricted to School or majors only until MONDAY, AUGUST 29th by noon.

If you received AP credit or transferred courses that is considered a pre-req, please go to Social Sciences Undergraduate Student Affairs Office located in 1201 SBSG to be cleared to enroll in the desired course.

For courses with a "B" or "X" restriction, please contact the instructor of the course.
